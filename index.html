      How We Help

      The Autism Support System is designed to empower children with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) by providing tools to enhance communication, build social skills, and manage daily routines. Our intuitive interface uses visual aids and text-to-speech to support non-verbal children, interactive scenarios to boost confidence in social settings, and customizable schedules to reduce anxiety and foster independence.
